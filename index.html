Systems Biology of Aging

Why individuals with similar genetic makeup live to different lifespans? How can we extend healthy lifespan? Our group studies these questions through mathematical modeling and computational analysis. We aim to develop predictive models from various genomics data sets based on gene/protein networks. Our group also develops various machine learning methods to studybiological big data.
